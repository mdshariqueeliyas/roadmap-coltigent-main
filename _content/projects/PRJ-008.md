# Executive Summary
With single-source battery contracts posing a board-level risk, establishing a formal risk management framework is critical. This initiative will help mitigate supply chain disruptions and ensure continuity in production, which is vital for meeting customer demand.

## Deliverables
- [ ] Identify and evaluate backup suppliers
- [ ] Develop formal risk triggers for supply chain events
- [ ] Create a risk management communication plan
- [ ] Establish regular review checkpoints for risk status
