# Executive Summary
Thin staffing during weekend shifts leads to operational inefficiencies and delays. By optimizing alerts and prioritizing actionable information, we can ensure that technicians are better equipped to handle issues as they arise, thus improving overall productivity.

## Deliverables
- [ ] Develop a prioritized alert system for weekend shifts
- [ ] Create a staffing plan that aligns with alert triggers
- [ ] Train staff on new alert protocols
- [ ] Monitor and adjust alert effectiveness post-implementation
