# Executive Summary
With single-source battery contracts posing a board-level risk, establishing a supplier performance and risk dashboard is crucial. This initiative will allow for better visibility into supplier risks, enabling proactive management and ensuring that critical components are sourced reliably as EV production ramps up.

## Deliverables
- [ ] Develop a supplier risk assessment framework
- [ ] Create a centralized dashboard for supplier performance metrics
- [ ] Implement alerts for late deliveries impacting production
