# Executive Summary
Fragmented recall traceability and quality data hinder effective compliance and risk management. A unified quality event log will streamline documentation processes, enhance audit readiness, and improve response times to quality issues, ultimately reducing rework and enhancing product reliability.

## Deliverables
- [ ] Design a centralized quality event logging system
- [ ] Integrate quality data from all relevant systems
- [ ] Train teams on usage and compliance requirements
